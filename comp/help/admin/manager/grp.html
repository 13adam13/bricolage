Groups

The permissions of a Group determine the privileges of its members. From this section you can create new Groups and edit the permissions of existing ones.
Search: Searches active Groups by name. Do a blank search to list all Groups. You can also call up a list of Groups by letter or by Type.
Add a New Group: Click this button to create a new Group. It brings you to the Group Profile page, where you will enter the Group's name and desired Group Type, then choose which permissions to grant it.
Group Manager: Click Edit to modify a Group's profile. Click Log to view the account's history of actions. To delete a Group, check the Delete box, scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Delete Checked button.
