This is where you add, edit, or delete Keywords.

Search
Searches active keywords by Keyword Name. You can also click one of the letters or 'All'.
Add a New Keyword
Click this button to create a new keyword. It brings you to the Keyword Profile page. Enter the requested information and then click Save at the bottom of the page.
Existing Keywords
Click Edit to modify a keyword. Click Log to view the account's history of actions. To delete a keyword, check the Delete box, then scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Delete Checked button.
