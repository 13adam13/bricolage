Properties
Enter the name of the Group along with a brief description of its role.
Group Type
Use the pulldown to select the Group's Type.

Click Next for the second page of the Profile.

Members
Determine which of the available users will be members of the Group. Select one at a time from the list on the left and click Add to List. The list on the right shows all of the Group's current members. To remove one, select the name and click Remove from List.
Permissions
Click this button to reach the Group's permissions page.

When finished, click Save.
