With this feature, a permitted administrator can publish all stories or media in selected categories.

On the first page, use Search to narrow down the list of categories as you normally would. Enter part of a URI and click Search. For each of the listed categories, you can now check the checkboxes for stories or media in any of those categories that you want to publish. Click 'Publish Checked' when you're ready to publish (or schedule publishing in the job queue).
