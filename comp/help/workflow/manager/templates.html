Find Templates

With this option you can search all Desks for an existing Templates.
Simple Search: Enter a title, URL or keyword and click Search. For a complete list of Templates, leave the space blank and click Search.
Advanced Search: This search allows you to be more specific about the Templates you're trying to find. Fill in any or all of the fields and click Search. To restart your search with a fresh page, click Clear Values.
Templates Found: Click View next to the desired Template to view its profile. If the Profile is not checked out to another user, you can fill in the Checkout box and click Checkout at the bottom of the page to move it to your Workspace. If you have already checked it out, click Edit to access its profile.
Create a New Template: This link will get you to the New Template page.
