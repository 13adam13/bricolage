New Template

Use this tool to customize your Bricolage. Here you will create Templates to include in Story and Media Profiles, increasing your options and molding the publishing tool to suit your needs. Click New Template from the left navbar and enter the following vital information:
Element: To base your new Template on an existing Element, scroll through the list and select one.
Generic: To create a new Template from scratch, check this box.
Output Channel: The destination for the Template, once it is deployed.
Category: Where on the site the Template will be deployed.
Priority: Tells users how urgent the Template is.
*Note: Priority must be filled in order to save.
When finished, click Create. This will bring you to the Template Profile page.
