My Workspace

This is your home base. Here you will store all the content items you create before checking them in to a Desk. You'll also be able to check items out from other Desks to edit them at your Workspace.

Click My Workspace in the upper left hand corner to return to this screen at any time.

Content Items

You can manage three different types of content in your Workspace: Media, Stories, and Templates. Newly created content items will reside in your Workspace until you move them -or check them in- to a Desk.

By checking an item into a Desk, you allow other permitted users to access it. Desks are like stations in an assembly line - an item needs to be approved by each one before it is ready to ship out to consumers.

For example: let's say you have created a new article for your Website. You check it in to the Edit Desk, where an editor examines its structure and accuracy. The editor then checks the article in to the Legal Desk, where a lawyer makes sure it will not get you sued. Next it goes to the Copy Desk, to be scrutinized for grammar and spelling. And finally, it is checked in to the Publish Desk, where you can push it to the site.

At each of these points, the person who needs to edit the item will check it out, or move it to his or her Workspace. When an item on a Desk is checked out to a user, it remains listed on that Desk and other users can still view it, but only the person who has checked it out can edit it.

Content Item Properties (on Workspace)

Title: Click to preview the item in another window (except for Templates).
Category: Where on the site an item is published.
Cover Date: The date that will be posted with the Story. (this is not necessarily the publish date, but what appears to viewers to be the publish date).
Priority: Tells users how urgent the item is.
Check in to: Moves the item to the listed Desk.
Check in to (pull down): Select the desired Desk, scroll to the bottom of the page and click Check In Assets. You can check in more than one item at a time.
Notes: View any editorial notes users have attached to the item.
Trail: Lists what Desks the item has been checked in to.
Edit: Modify the item or its profile.
Log: Displays the item's history of actions.
Delete: Check the Delete box, scroll to the bottom of the page and click Delete Checked. You can delete more than one item at a time.
