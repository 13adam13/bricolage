Media Profile Data

Here you can upload the Media to the file, adjust its vital information and enter Contributors. Be sure to click "Save" at the bottom of the page to save the Media file to your Workspace.

1) Information

Notes: Attach editorial notes to the Media. The Notes will be viewable to other users when you check the Media in to a Desk but will not show up when you publish it.
Trail: Lists what Desks the Media has been checked in to (None if it is a new Media).
ID: The item's identification number, given automatically.
Asset Type: The type of Media of the file, such as Photograph or Illustration.
Version: Indicates how many times the Media has been revised.
Title: The name of the Media file.
Description: A brief description of the Media.
Source: The party that has provided the Media.
Priority: Tells users how urgent the Media is.
*Note: Priority and Title fields must be filled in order to save.
Cover Date: The date that will be posted with the media. (this is not necessarily the publish date, but what appears to viewers to be the publish date).
Expire Date: The date and time the Media will be taken offline.

2) Upload a File

File Path: Upload the Media. Click Browse, find the file on your computer or shared drive, and select it. Its path will display in the field. Click Upload to attach it to the Bricolage Media file. Only one Media can be attached to each Bricolage file; if you upload a second Media, it will replace the first.
Download: Once a Media is uploaded, any user with access to the Media profile can download it. Click the hyperlink to open the file in a new window, then save it.

3) Contributors

Enter the names of the item's producers, including any photographer or illustrator who was involved in the creation of the Media. Click Edit to get to the Contributor Association page. Under Choose Contributors, find the person's name and click Associate. On the next page, select his or her role and click Next. When done, click Save.
Back on the Media Profile page, save the Media to your Workspace by clicking Save.
